Who we are ?

At Rukatech Computer College, our journey began with a shared passion for web development and a drive to create meaningful digital solutions. What started as a learning project among a group of dedicated students quickly grew into a full-fledged vision. Motivated by real-world challenges in the real estate industry, we set out to build a platform that simplifies property buying, selling, and renting — all through a modern, user-friendly interface.

Fueled by curiosity, teamwork, and the skills we gained through our courses, we developed a full-stack real estate website that connects clients with properties seamlessly. Our platform showcases the power of web technologies while reflecting our commitment to innovation and quality. This project is not just a website — it’s a testament to what passion, collaboration, and continuous learning can achieve.
